Rob Sorbo's Hobbies

There are two hobbies that get me the most excited -- traveling and football.

(It's been a hard 26 years since the mid-90s for Cowboys fans!). Over the years,
I've had several chances to go see several NFL games. One of my bucket-list dreams
is to go see a game in every NFL stadium.

Here is a list of stadiums I've been to so far:

  • Levi's Stadium in Santa Clara, California
  • Lambeau Field in Green Bay, Wisconsin
  • Heinz Field in Pittsburh, Pennsylvania
