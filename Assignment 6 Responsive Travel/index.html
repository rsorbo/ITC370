Explore Beautiful Budapest


Often called the "Paris of the East," Budapest is the capital city of Hungary. Conquered by both Nazi Germany to the West and USSR to the East, Budapest has history of war and resilience. Since the fall of the Iron Curtain, Budapest has thrived. Growing into the ninth largest city in the EU and an economic hub.

Gaze at the old-meets-new architecture, capstoned by the brilliant Parliament Building. Take a sunset cruise down the Danube River, and marvel at the beauty of Budapest at night. Dip in the baths and spas. Cross the Danube, and experience the beauty of the Budapest skyline from the Fisherman's Bastion. Eat goulash and with a Hungarian beer. No matter what you want to do, you can do it in Budpaset!
