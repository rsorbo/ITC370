About Me

Rob Sorbo

I am a Customer Success Manager at Ion Wave Technologies, a web-based SaaS used by
many government agencies for their procurement. In my role as Customer Success Manager,
I provide support and training to my customers, as well as implementing new customers.
I love the teaching aspect of my job! At the encouragement of my boss, I have begun
training to become a developer.

Since the SaaS I work for is web-based, it will be important for me to learn aspects
of web design. However, the primary tools I'll use are more back-end, such as C#, SQL,
and .Net. I have already taken a C# course, and I really enjoyed it. I have worked with
many databases in the past, so I have some knowledge of SQL as well.

One of the things I've loved the most about my job is training our customers. I lead
weekly webinars, and I've received many compliments about my teaching. I'm not totally
sure where my path will take me, but if this whole computer programmer thing works out,
I think I'd like to continue my studies to the graduate level so that I can some day
teach CS.
